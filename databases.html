Medical Datasets

  • Kermany Datasets https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/rscbjbr9sj/2

    A large collection of retinal OCT images for various eye conditions.

  • Harvard Glaucoma Datasets https://github.com/Harvard-Ophthalmology-AI-Lab/Harvard-GDP

    A comprehensive dataset for glaucoma diagnosis and progression analysis.

  • OASIS Datasets https://sites.wustl.edu/oasisbrains/

    Open Access Series of Imaging Studies for brain imaging.

  • APTOS 2019 https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/aptos2019-blindness-detection

    Dataset for detecting diabetic retinopathy.

  • DRIVE Dataset https://drive.grand-challenge.org/

    Digital Retinal Images for Vessel Extraction, used for vessel segmentation.

  • STARE Dataset https://cecas.clemson.edu/~ahoover/stare/

    Structured analysis of retina dataset for vessel segmentation and disease detection.

  • REFUGE Dataset https://refuge.grand-challenge.org/

    Retinal Fundus Glaucoma Challenge dataset for glaucoma detection.
